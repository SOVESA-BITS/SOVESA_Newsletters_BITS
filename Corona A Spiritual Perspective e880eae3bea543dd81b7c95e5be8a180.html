Any virus causes panic, tension, distress, and fear of death in human society. We all have a basic instinct to save ourselves.

No one wants to die...
Everyone has a fear of death...

We see people wearing masks, using sanitizer, avoiding travels to safeguard from the clutches of cruel teethes of death, and they name it Coronavirus now. But to escape from death was never possible for any living being.

The great logicians, scientists, and anyone we can name who tried to save themselves or human society were never successful. Trying to protect from SARS or CORONA or any other disease is a method to save ourselves for a temporary period, but then again, the time will tick, and we need to go away. The more strictly we are trying against nature’s design, the more bounce back we have to bear.

Living entity keeps on dying and getting new births. CORONAVIRUS causes death, and we saw this cruel face in many births. In each birth, we tried to save ourselves, but we were frustrated.

In Bhagavad Gītā, Lord Kr̥ṣṇa says,

duḥkhālayam aśāśvatam
This material world is temporary and full of miseries.

Our fear is not from CORONA; our fear is

“I do not want to leave this world,” “What will happen to my family, my business, my followers, my popularity.”

Scriptures remind us,

uttiṣṭhata jāgrata prāpya varān nibodhata
Wake up and take advantage of the human form of life.

Because we are sleeping, forgetting our real home Vaikuṇṭha. This is the ultimate destination according to the Vedic scriptures. vigata kuṇṭha iti vaikuṇṭha. It means a place where no anxiety or miseries exist. Once we reach that ultimate destination or param dhāma, we will not come back as stated in Gītā: yad gatvā na nivartante
